Samsung S26: What's Actually New vs. What's Marketing

consumer-tech

Samsung announced the S26 this week. I’ve been looking through the specs and early reviews. Here’s what’s real versus what’s hype.

The Actual Improvements

Camera hardware got a bump. The main sensor is larger. Low-light performance should be noticeably better based on the sensor size alone. Everything else is computational photography improvements, which Samsung’s been good at for years.

Battery is slightly bigger. This translates to maybe 10-15% more real-world use. Not game-changing, but welcome.
