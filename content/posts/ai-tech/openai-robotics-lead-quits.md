# OpenAI Robotics Lead Quits Over Pentagon Deal

## The Story

Caitlin Kalinowski, head of OpenAI's robotics team, has resigned in response to the company's controversial Pentagon agreement. This marks another high-profile departure as AI companies navigate military partnerships.

## Key Takeaways

- Kalinowski's departure signals internal tension over AI-military ties
- Pentagon recently classified Anthropic as a 'supply chain risk'
- Google making Gmail/Drive easier for AI agents to use

## What This Means

For the AI industry: Expect more scrutiny on military contracts.
For developers: Agentic AI tools are getting more powerful.
For the future: The line between AI companies and defense continues to blur.
